Google Analytics:

- Enter GA ID in the <script> tag of your index.html file. Replace 'Enter you here GA ID' with your actual GA ID.

Cookie Consent:

- Initialize Google Analytics with the GA ID in the CookieConsentBanner.jsx component. Replace 'Enter here you GA ID' with your actual GA ID.

EmailJS:

- In the Contact form component, replace 'here you serviceID' with your EmailJS Service ID.
- Replace 'template ID' with your EmailJS Template ID.
- Replace 'userID' with your EmailJS User ID.
